How It Started

Built for the ones who actually go.

Travel & LIV Collective was born from a simple frustration: too many group chats full of "we should travel!" but nobody ever actually books it.

We built this for the ones who do. The ones who crave more than just a destination — they want a shared experience with people who truly get it. Real connection, curated environments, and the kind of memories that don't need a filter.

From rooftop nights to beach villas, every trip we curate is built around one thing: bringing like-minded people together in extraordinary places. Mid-luxury stays, welcome dinners, farewell celebrations, and experiences you genuinely won't find on Google.
