💳 Booking & Payments

  1. On the booking page, select your desired room type.
  2. Click 'Book Now' to fill out the pre-booking form.
  3. After completing the form, you'll be taken to checkout automatically.
  4. Fill in your details and make the required payment.
  5. Your reservation is confirmed on receipt of the booking confirmation email.
  6. Fill in the 'Booking Details' form on your WeTravel account as soon as possible to finalise your booking.

Yes! You can secure your spot with a non-refundable deposit and then pay the remaining balance in monthly instalments. The number and amount of instalments varies by trip and room type — check the individual trip page for the exact payment schedule.

Deposits are non-refundable and vary by room type. Full payment options are also available. See the Upcoming Trips page for current pricing details.

We typically offer two room types:

  • Shared Room: For people travelling solo or with a friend/partner who wish to share. Cost-effective and a great way to meet people.
  • Solo Room: For people travelling solo who want their own space. Single occupancy only — cannot be booked for two people.

Some trips (like Zanzibar) may also offer different villa categories — e.g. Garden View or Beachfront.

If travelling solo, you'll be paired with another TravelNLiv guest of the same gender. We use a roommate matching process to ensure compatible pairings. If travelling with a friend or partner, you can request to room together — we recommend booking 2 spaces and selecting 2 for quantity.

The initial deposit is non-refundable. For any questions about cancellations, changes, or extenuating circumstances, please contact us directly at hello@travelnliv.com and we'll do our best to assist.

🏝️ Zanzibar 2025

Yes — it depends on the departure week. Week 1 (11–18 July) is for ages 27–35 only. Week 2 (21–28 July) follows our standard age range of 21–35.

Flights should be booked to Zanzibar Abeid Amani Karume International Airport (ZNZ). From the UK, airlines including British Airways, Qatar Airways, Emirates, Oman Air, Ethiopian Airlines, Kenya Airways and Turkish Airlines all serve this route. Please land before 12pm on Day 1 to be eligible for the included airport transfer.

Most foreign passport holders need a tourist visa to enter Zanzibar. You can apply for an eVisa in advance at visa.immigration.go.tz. Visa on arrival is available for British passport holders and some other nationalities. Please note we cannot assist with visa applications.

Yes — travel insurance is mandatory. The Zanzibar government also requires all visitors to have inbound travel insurance (ZIC), costing USD $44 per person. This covers some medical emergencies and travel disruptions. This can be purchased at inbound.visitzanzibar.go.tz. Your general travel insurance must also cover water-related activities.
